Usage Instructions

  1. Download the PNG files you need
  2. Import your gameplay footage into your video editor (CapCut, Adobe Premiere, etc.)
  3. Add the PNG files as layers above your gameplay footage
  4. Position and time them according to your script
  5. Add text animations for dynamic content

For more detailed instructions, refer to the TikTok Content Production Guide.
